# Simanga's DeutschTrainer

A comprehensive, interactive web app for preparing for the **Goethe-Zertifikat A2** exam. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no backend, just open it and start learning.

**[Live App](https://SudoAdeline.github.io/simanga-deutsch)**

---

## Features

### Dashboard
- Countdown to exam day (April 30, 2026)
- Stats at a glance: words learned, verbs mastered, streak days
- Goethe A2 exam structure overview (Lesen, Hören, Schreiben, Sprechen)

### Prüfungsvorbereitung (Exam Prep)
- Practice for all 4 exam sections in real exam format
- **Lesen**: Newspaper texts, info boards, emails, ad matching
- **Hören**: Radio texts, interview questions
- **Schreiben**: SMS and formal email tasks with word counters
- **Sprechen**: Interview topics and prompt cards

### Wortschatz (Vocabulary)
- **204 words** from the official Goethe A2 word list
- 10 categories: Familie, Essen, Wohnen, Reisen, Alltag, Arbeit, Freizeit, Körper, Schule, Zeit
- Flashcards with spaced-repetition scoring
- Filterable word list (by category, learned/unlearned)
- Add your own custom vocabulary

### Verben Trainer (Verb Training)
- **35+ verbs** — regular, irregular, modal, and separable
- Conjugation drills for Präsens, Präteritum, and Perfekt
- Interactive flashcard practice and quizzes

### Grammatik (Grammar)
- **Grammar Hit List**: Futur I, Modalverben, Fälle, Adjektivendungen
- **Level 2 Exercises** (74+ items):
  - Negation (kein vs. nicht)
  - Conjunctions (dass, weil, wie, wenn)
  - Pronouns (Nominativ, Akkusativ, Dativ)
  - Possessives (mein/dein/sein + formal Sie)
  - Question words + Wieviel
  - sein/haben conjugation drill
  - Time expressions (am, im, um)
  - Pronunciation (ie vs. ei)
  - Error correction
- **15+ quiz questions** covering grammar rules and word order (TeKaMoLo)

### Phrasen & Alltag (Phrases & Everyday)
- **89 phrases** across 8 situations: greetings, shopping, restaurant, directions, doctor, phone, opinions, small talk
- Formal vs. informal distinctions
- Context notes for each phrase

### Schreiben Üben (Writing Practice)
- 3 informal message tasks (SMS/WhatsApp, 20–30 words)
- 3 formal email tasks (30–40 words)
- Built-in word counter and example answers

### Sprechen Üben (Speaking Practice)
- 8 speaking topics with guided prompts
- 16 randomized prompt cards with hints
- 6 planning scenarios (birthday, cooking, party, etc.)

### Sentence Puzzles
- 20+ sentence reconstruction exercises
- Covers present tense, future, modal verbs, subordinate clauses, W-questions, and TeKaMoLo word order

### Fun Zone
- Word games and entertainment-focused learning
